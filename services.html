OUR SERVICES

We offer a range of courses, all in keeping with our need, as a collective, to preserve the planet, animals and humanity. Our courses range from growing food, permaculture, building solar panels/wind turbines, to making and mending clothing. We will also be introducing bee hives, goats and chickens. At LIH, we believe in the importance of survival skills and have created a company that will arm you with the knowledge, you need, to live with the planet, in harmony!

Happily, our location, will allow us to offer cycling tours and in the future, walking tours and mountain climbing, for the more daring adventurer.
