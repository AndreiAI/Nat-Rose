ABOUT US

Live in Harmony (LIH) came about from the need to create a system that works with the planet. We aim to create a lifestyle where people cycle, learn to grow food, make/source 'free' energy, mend/make clothing and keep bees. We believe life should be about learning, education and exploration, while achieving one's goals, but not at the expense of the planet and/or others.

We offer a range of courses to educate people (for free) how to work with the planet by teaching about the environment, sustainability, permaculture and green energy. At LIH, we pride ourselves on giving back and understand how important it is to preserve our beautiful planet.

Our team has been carefully selected and is not limited to:

  • Designers
  • Cyclists
  • Food Connoisseurs
  • Environmentalists
  • Animal Lovers
  • Engineers

We enjoy what we do and hope you will too!
